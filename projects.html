What is
HAARP?

HAARP is an Audio NFT Marketplace.

That will let you own unique and exclusive music NFTs under the BSC network, allowing to exchange songs with your friends or resell it to the community, for every transaction made, the original artist will get direct royalties.

With all the available music streams, HAARP doesn't pretend to reach the masses, we want you to own a exclusive song of an artist, you could be one of the only few persons to listen to a song unless you sell it.

Trying to revive that old habit that was buying and album and just listening to it, not as background but buying a piece of art.
